Insertion Sort

When we talk about ordination, we try to order a sequence of data in descending or ascending direction.

Imagine you're holding one deck of cards in one hand and you want to sort them into your other hand. You start with one card, considering it sorted. Then, you take the next card and insert it into the correct position relative to the first one, so they are sorted together.
