Queues

This type of structure is something like a line of people waiting to order something in a coffee shop. The first person in line is the first to get served.

Queues are useful to process data in the order they were added, following the FIFO (First-In-First-Out) principle.

You may notice that "enqueue" means to add an element at the end of the queue.

However, "dequeue" means to remove the element which is at the beginning of the queue.
